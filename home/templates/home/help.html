Getting Started

How do I create an account?
Click on either "Manufacturer Register" or "Supplier Register" on the homepage, depending on your role. Fill out the registration form with your details and follow the instructions to complete your account setup. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account before you can start using the platform.
What's the difference between manufacturer and supplier accounts?
Manufacturers are companies looking for suppliers to provide materials, components, or services for their production needs. Suppliers are companies offering materials, components, or specialized services to manufacturers. Each account type has tailored features and dashboards designed for their specific business requirements.

Account Management

How do I reset my password?
On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your registered email address. You'll receive instructions to reset your password within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see the email in your inbox. The reset link expires after 24 hours for security.
Can I change my account type after registration?
Account types cannot be changed after registration due to security and data integrity reasons. If you need a different account type, you'll need to create a new account with the correct type. Contact our support team if you need assistance transferring important data from your old account.

Platform Features

How do I find suppliers/manufacturers?
After logging in, use the advanced search functionality in your dashboard to find potential partners. You can filter by industry, location, company size, certifications, and other criteria. Use our matching algorithm to find the most compatible partners based on your specific requirements.
Is there a messaging system?
Yes, we provide a comprehensive built-in messaging system for secure communication with your partners. You can share documents, send quotes, track conversation history, and set up automated notifications. All communications are encrypted and stored securely on our platform.
