Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform and services. We're here to help you succeed.

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click on either "Manufacturer Register" or "Supplier Register" on the homepage, depending on your role. Fill out the registration form with your details and follow the instructions to complete your account setup.

What's the difference between manufacturer and supplier accounts?

Manufacturers are companies looking for suppliers to provide materials or components. Suppliers are companies offering materials, components, or services to manufacturers.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all new users. You can explore all platform features during this period without any commitment. No credit card is required to start your free trial.

Account Management

How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your registered email address. You'll receive instructions to reset your password via email.

Can I change my account type after registration?

No, account types cannot be changed after registration. You'll need to create a new account with the correct type. However, you can use the same email address for both account types if needed.

How do I update my company information?

You can update your company information by navigating to the "Account Settings" section in your dashboard. Click on "Company Profile" to make changes to your business details, contact information, and other relevant data.

Platform Features

How do I find suppliers/manufacturers?

After logging in, use the search functionality in your dashboard to find potential partners. You can filter by industry, location, certifications, and other criteria to find the best matches for your business needs.

Is there a messaging system?

Yes, once you've connected with a partner through the platform, you can use our built-in messaging system to communicate securely. The messaging system allows you to exchange files, track conversations, and manage all your communications in one place.

Can I integrate this with my existing ERP system?

Yes, our platform offers API integration with most major ERP systems. We support integration with SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics, and other popular ERP solutions. Contact our support team for specific integration assistance.

Still have questions?

Our dedicated support team is ready to help you with any additional questions you may have. We're committed to your success on our platform.
